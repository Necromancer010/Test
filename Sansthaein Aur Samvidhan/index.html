Sansthaein Aur Samvidhan

Purpose

The "Sansthaein Aur Samvidhan" project aims to enhance constitutional literacy among citizens through engaging and interactive games. By simplifying the Constitution of India, focusing on the Legislature, Executive, and Judiciary, we seek to make constitutional knowledge accessible to all.

Constitutional Journey (Board Game)

This game takes players through a journey to explore key aspects of the Indian Constitution in a fun, board-game format. Players roll a dice and land on different spots with questions, challenges, or mini-games related to a particular section of the Constitution. Correct answers allow them to move forward; wrong answers might send them back or impose penalties. Mini-games include puzzles, matching the correct articles to constitutional topics, or short quizzes.
